About me

Since Septembre 2019 I am a postdoctoral researcher jointly at the École Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay and at EDF, working with Vianney Perchet and Olivier Beaude on learning in repeated auctions with applications to smart grids.

Before that, I was a teaching and research associate at Université Paris Dauphine.

I completed my Ph.D. in applied mathematics at the Toulouse School of Economics under the supervision of Jérôme Renault.
